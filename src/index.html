Obsidian

Obsidian is the first GraphQL caching library for the Deno runtime. Built in the Deno runtime with Typescript, it allows a developer to easily utilize a redis cache to improve the response times for server requests. Grabbing a GraphQL response from cache can easily decrease the time it takes to get your client information by two orders of magnitude or more!

Wordle Plus!

A simple but addicting rendition of the viral game "Wordle" that ups the ante by adding a 6 letter to the secret word. Built with React and MaterialUI, the game is played by guessing a 6 letter word and hitting enter. Letters in your word that are in the secret word will turn yellow and if they are in the correct location they will turn green. If it is not in the word it will stay gray. Try to guess the secret word in less than 7 tries!
